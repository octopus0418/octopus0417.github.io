Idea

The theme of the art competition was "Together"，a word brimming with warmth. So I envisioned a palette of softly saturated colors to echo the harmony between children across the strait, with playful, childlike interactions and a dreamy sense of floating in the air. To enrich the composition, I chose balloons,it means symbols of a vibrant childhood，and dolphins, representing the spanning waters between shores

Material

This painting was completed in 2023 while I was practicing fine arts for the DSE.

The entire piece was executed in acrylics, and to save time(because We want to preparing the DSE) I enhanced the details with colored pencils.
